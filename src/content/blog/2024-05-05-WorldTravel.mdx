6. **Amalfi Coast, Italy**: Imagine driving along winding coastal roads, past colorful villages clinging to cliffs, with the azure Mediterranean Sea below. The Amalfi Coast offers just that, along with delicious Italian cuisine and charming towns like Positano and Ravello.
